# 🎨 Prompt para Imagem de Capa — LinkedIn / Reddit

## Sugestão de estilo

Uma imagem que mostre visualmente o conceito: **9 perspectivas diferentes convergindo em uma única compreensão integrada**. O estilo ideal é algo entre diagrama conceitual e arte generativa — sofisticado, limpo, que passe a ideia de inteligência, cognição, multiplicidade.

---

## Prompt 1 (Recomendado — mais conceitual e clean)

**Funciona em:** Midjourney, DALL-E 3, Stable Diffusion, Leonardo AI, Bing Image Creator

```
A minimalist conceptual artwork showing 9 luminous orbs of different colors arranged in a circle, each with a unique symbolic icon inside: a book (linguistic), a geometric pattern (logical-mathematical), a cube (spatial), a dancing figure (kinesthetic), a music note (musical), connected dots (interpersonal), a mirror (intrapersonal), a tree (naturalistic), and a galaxy (existential). From each orb, a thin beam of light converges toward the center where a bright diamond core is forming. Dark background with deep purple and navy tones. Clean lines, subtle glow effects, sophisticated aesthetic. No text. 4K, elegant, modern, scientific yet artistic.
```

---

## Prompt 2 (Alternativo — mais dramático, "cérebro")

**Funciona em:** Midjourney, DALL-E 3, Stable Diffusion

```
A stylized human brain silhouette floating in dark space, made of interconnected glowing neural networks in purple and gold. From 9 different points around the brain, beams of colored light converge into a single bright point at the center. Each beam has tiny symbolic elements floating along it: letters, numbers, geometric shapes, musical notes, organic patterns. Cinematic lighting, volumetric fog, deep blue background with subtle stars. Ethereal, sophisticated, scientific visualization style. 8K.
```

---

## Prompt 3 (Mais abstrato/artístico — para destaque no feed)

**Funciona em:** Midjourney, DALL-E 3

```
Abstract generative art: 9 colored rings floating in dark space, each with a unique texture and color palette — warm gold, cool blue, vibrant purple, emerald green, coral red, soft pink, deep indigo, forest green, cosmic violet. From each ring, streams of particles flow inward toward a brilliant white center where a crystalline structure is forming. Fluid dynamics, particle systems, volumetric light. Sophisticated color harmony. No text. Moody atmospheric background. Artistic, contemporary, would look good as a LinkedIn banner.
```
